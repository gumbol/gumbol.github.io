Display monitor can be broken into three functions

  • The width and length of an image (resolution)
  • The accuracy of the image (pixels per inch)
  • The smoothnes of the image (frames per second)

Frames per second (FPS)

Motion pictures are a series of pictures or images that collectevly create the illusion of movment. a single picture is 1 frame. More frames per second translate to smoother motion.

What FPS to go with?

Monitors support varies. It ranges from 32 to 126 FPS and its measured in MHz. A good FPS would be 32.

Pixels

Pixels are the unit used to measure an image dimension in the digital realm. There is no physical reference for a pixel. A 2 pixels in one screen can be different in another.

Aspect ratio is the ratio an image is taken at. Key points to understand about aspect ratio:

  • The image will always be distorted in some way if the aspect ratio of the image is different to the screen's native aspect ratio.
  • To maintain the aspect ratio of an image, screens will keep the original aspect ratio and fill out the extra space with black pillars on the sides or above and below the image.
What to do?

When buying a monitor make sure the screen native aspect ratio is one that is widely used. for example a 16:9 aspect ratio is currently the popular aspect ratio used by many content developers and movie productions.

Resolution

Resolution is the amout of pixels there are in the screen. but alone it does not show how accurate an image can be. that is why pixels are mapped into inches to give it a physical dimension. In other words we can say that 1 inch has x amount of pixels. That is why we need PPI

Pixels per inch

What determine an image quality is the pixel density. This goes hand in hand with the resolution number since A good number is 110-120ppi

i listed the numbers for the 27' and 32' display monitors

2560x1440 -> 108.79ppi

32' Display should support a resolution of
3200x1800 -> 114.73ppi
3840x2160 -> 137.68ppi

Frame rate

This is the amount of images displayed per second. A good range is 60-126Mhz
