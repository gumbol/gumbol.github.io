Building a practical CSS style

I want this blog post to be an intro to fundamental styles that can apply to any website. To do that i will start off by how websites differ from real sites. One of the limiting factors of websites is that they have little space to work with. This is specially true for mobile screens. Another factor is that you cannot engage with content with all your senses. you cannot smell things you cannot feel the full 3d experience of walking into a shop. First thing first, since most blog content is text based. I will start off with a good approach to styling text content, then i will move on to styling images and other media.
