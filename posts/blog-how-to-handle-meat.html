How to clean up after handling meat product

We all know that you need to clean your hands before and after handling any type of meat. But how can you hold the soap bottle after you have handled the meat?

One way is before you handle the meat add some drops of soap on the counter top. That way you can just swap your hand on the soap without contaminating the soap bottle.
