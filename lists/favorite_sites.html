List of some of my Favorite Sites

Wikipedia An online encyclopedia.
Internet Archive A nonprofit digital library offering free universal access to books, movies; music. They also has what is called a waybackmachine that has over 446 billion archived web pages!
Khan Academy A nonprofit with a mission of providing a free, world-class education for anyone anywhere.
Librivox A website that host free public domain audiobooks read by volunteers from around the world (you can be one if you wish!).
Mayo Clinic
 A nonprofit academic medical center that has a mission to inspire hope and contribute to health and well being.
Scratch
 A block based programming language and website.
Snap! A block based programming language. An advance form of Scratch
