This is a simple recipe for baking fries till crispy!. Now crispy is a subjective matter but no matter! this recipe will get it crisp

Ingredients
2 medium potatoes sliced with skin on
2 tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Preparation
In a bowl mix all the ingredients together until the fries are coated (very important!)
Now i have read many recipes on how much time and what temp is needed to make them crispy. But they are all too technical. I heat the oven to 350F (175C) and let it cook for about 40min. The fries in the picture were bleached so i did around 30min. They came out not wet nor super dry they were firm. This is how i like my crispy, feel free to let them bake for another 5-10min!
