Roasted chicken

This is a favorite of mine. It is simple yet packed with flavor!. you only need a chicken (butterflied) some mixed herbs, what i used in the following dish had around 16 blends including garlic and onions! (if you have any mix herbs I recommended sticking to ones that have less than 16 blends.) but use whatever you feel like using.  Some dry red pepper and salt. It is simple yet flavorful!.


Ingredients:
  • whole chicken butterflied (flattened out)
  • 1.5 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dry red pepper (use gloves when handling these or the heat will stick to your fingers for a long while)


Preparation

  1. Go ahead and butterfly the chicken

  2. mix the rest of the ingredients together then rub the whole chicken

  3. Let it rest in the fridge for at least 45 minutes.


    4. This is the chicken after marinating in the fridge for about 1 hour






  4. Turn the oven to 350F(175C). Put the chicken in an oven pan and cover with foil

  5. Cook for around 45 minutes (you do not want it to cook too quickly)

  6. Remove the foil and turn on the grill (the ceiling of the oven) to crisp the skin. around 5-10 minutes

    7. This is after it was cooked.





    This is after it was grilled.





    Break it.





    Enjoy it! you can eat it with bread or cook some rice on the side (The stuff under the spoon is called Saurekraut in case you are wondering.)
